Why HackSource?

dashboard

Organization

When you find a great resource you want to share it. But on most social media, it's only a matter of minutes before your post is lost in the feed. HackSource gives you a dedicated and organized place to share and store valuable resources.

Usefulness Ranking

With the abundance of information on the web, finding resources can feel like looking for a needle in a haystack. That's why we devised a voting system to ensure that the best resources always rise to the top of your search results.
